Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 02/19/2025
Thank you for using 4:20 ("App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our App to check when the next 4:20 occurs around the world.

Information We Collect

Our App is designed to function entirely on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The App only accesses:
  • The current time on your device
  • Your device’s time zone settings
  • No other data is accessed, stored, or shared in any way.

How We Use Your Information

The time and time zone data from your device is used only to calculate and display when the next 4:20 will occur somewhere in the world.
  • This information is not stored
  • This information is not sent to any server
  • This information is not shared with any third parties

Data Storage and Security

  • Local Access Only: The App does not save any information to your device’s storage.
  • No Remote Access: We do not have access to your device, and no data is transmitted externally.
  • Security: Since no data is stored or transmitted, the only security measures applicable are those you maintain on your device.

Third-Party Services

Our App does not use or integrate with any third-party services. All functionality runs directly on your device without external dependencies.

Children’s Privacy

Our App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you believe a child has used the App inappropriately, please contact us.
